Stack of FreeRTOS
---

+ v10

```
Stack Layout:

    Low |----------------------|
        |          $FPU        | ( configSUPPORT_FPU )
        |----------------------|
        |         Dummy word   | ( Dummy word for 8-byte stack pointer alignment )
        |----------------------|
        |          $IPC        |
        |----------------------|
        |          $IPSW       | ( configSUPPORT_ZOL )
        |----------------------|
        |          $LB         | (ZOL)
        |----------------------|
        |          $LE         | (ZOL)
        |----------------------|
        |          $LC         | (ZOL)
        |----------------------|
        |         $IFC_LP      | ( configSUPPORT_IFC )
        |----------------------|
        |          $R0         |
        |----------------------|
        |    .      |    .     |
        |    .      |    .     |
        | $R(10-n)  | $R(24-n) |
        |-----------|----------|
        |    $R10   | $R24     |
        |-----------|----------|
        |    $R15   | $R25     |
        | (Reduced) | (full)   |
        |----------------------|
        |          $R28 (FP)   |
        |----------------------|        ^
        |          $R29 (GP)   |        |
        |----------------------|        |
        |          $R30 (LP)   |
        |----------------------| ( Stack Pointer )
   High

```

Stack Layout:

        |          $SP          |
        |-----------------------|
        |          $PSW         |
        |-----------------------|
        |          $IPC         |

        |          $IPSW        |

        |          $LE          | (ZOL)
        |-----------------------|
        |          $LC          | (ZOL)
        |-----------------------|
        |          $IFC_LP      |
        |-----------------------|
        |             .lo       |
        |          $d1.hi       |
        |-----------------------|
        |             .lo       |
        |          $d0.hi       |
        |-----------------------|
        |          $R2          |
        |-----------------------|
        |    .      |   .       |
        |    .      |   .       |
        | $R(10-n)  | $R(26-n)  |
        |-----------|-----------|
        |    $R10   |  $R26     |
        |-----------|-----------|
        |    $R15   |  $R27     |
        | (Reduced) |  (full)   |

        |          $R28 (FP)    |

        |-----------------------|
        |          $R30 (LP)    |
        |-----------------------|        ^
        |          $R0          |        |
        |-----------------------|        |
        |          $R1          |
